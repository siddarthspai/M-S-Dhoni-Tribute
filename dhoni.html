M.S Dhoni

The man who made a billion dreams come true
M.S Dhoni watching the ball sail over long-on as India lift the world cup after 28 years-The Dream

    Here's a time line of M.S Dhoni's life:

  • 1981 - Born in Ranchi, Bihar.
  • 1995 - Regular Starter at Commando Cricket Club after impressing with his wicketkeeping and batting skills.
  • 2001 - Worked as a Travelling Ticket Examiner(TTE) at Kharagpur Railway Station.
  • 2003 - Picked in the India A Squad for a tour of Zimbabwe and Kenya after impressing in the Ranji Trophy-The Domestic Test League.
  • 2004 - Makes ODI debut against Bangladesh.Not off to a great start.Gets himself run-out without scoring.
  • 2005 - Scores 183 runs in an ODI against Sri Lanka-A Personal Best till now.Also makes Test Debut.
  • 2007 - Picked in the 2007 World Cup Squad.Ends in Disappointment as India go out in the group stages.Made T20I Captain for India heading into the 1st T20I Tournament organized by the ICC.Ends in Success as India win the Tournament.Dhoni becomes just the second Indian Captain to win an ICC Tournament(behind only the Great Kapil Dev)
  • 2008 - Bought by the Chennai Super Kings and made team captain in the Inaugural IPL.Very Successful at the club lifting 3 IPL trophies and a Champions League Trophy(as of 2018).
  • 2009 - Helps India achieve Number 1 status in the ICC World Test Rankings.
  • 2011 - Wins the ICC ODI World Cup in the final against Sri Lanka after scoring an unbeaten 91 runs.India lift the cup after a 28 year wait.
  • 2013 - Wins the ICC Champions Trophy held in England amid strong match-fixing allegations.
  • 2014 - Retires from Test Cricket following a string of poor performances and internal pressure.
  • 2015 - Captains India to a semi-final finish in the 2015 ICC ODI World Cup held in Australia and New Zealand.
  • 2017 - Resigns from Captaincy in ODI and T20I formats.Hands Captaincy over to Virat Kholi.Continues to remain a prominent figure in the dressing room.
